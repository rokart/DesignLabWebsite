Our services

Want to work with us? You can contact our Project Managers via an online form, or email on designlab@uk.ey.com. Once we know what you want, we’ll complete a Response to Brief (or RTB) document with you, outlining cost, schedule and the resources required. As you can see above, that’s where the work begins. So get in touch!

Learning

eLearning

We produce highly-engaging online learning courses, containing a variety of animations, knowledge checks and assessment sections. To align with cost and time constraints, we can create your eLearning to one of our standard templates, but we also offer bespoke eLearnings that can be customised to your every requirement.

VILTs

Our VILTs (Virtual Instructor-Led Training) are interactive webinars in which learners are guided through the session by a real person, in real time. As well as creating the course itself, we will write facilitator guides for the instructor to refer to throughout the session.

ILTs

We may be a Design Lab, but we haven't forgotten about traditional instructor-led training. As well as slide decks and facilitator guides, we design printed handouts and large-scale visual aids for learners and facilitators to use. We can also create digital aids, including augmented reality, to bring a different interactive experience into the classroom.



Audio Visual Production

Animations and Videobytes

With a team of animators, illustrators, digital artists and graphic designers, our design team can create anything from simple animated logos to complex motion graphics and narrated, animated videos. We can incorporate complex CGI and special effect and we have our own library of animated characters that you can select for your product. Both our animations and videobytes come in standard and advanced, according to the level of content you require.

Filming and Voiceover

Whether it’s an interview, voiceover or fully-narrated, informational videobyte, our video production specialist is on hand to make movie magic. Our studio boasts state-of-the-art cameras, lighting and audio recording equipment – including a separate recording booth. We’ve even got a clapper board.

Development and testing

Development

Using their industry-leading knowledge and technological ability, our Development team build web portals, SharePoint pages and fully-fledged, interactive websites to support your learning experience.

Testing

Every element of a digital product is rigorously tested by our QA team, ensuring both precision and quality for our clients.
