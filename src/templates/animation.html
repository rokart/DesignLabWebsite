Animation: Character Level 1 Basic Mobility //
GAL Project Management

Time

20 days

Cost

£4,150

Resources

  • Instructional Designer
  • Digital Designer
  • Proof reader/Editorial
  • Video/Audio expert
  • Adobe Flash
  • Adobe Character Animator
  • Adobe After Effects

Project overview

Our in-house animation team devised a project-specific character to integrate into our GAL offering, using programs such as Adobe Character Animator and Adobe Flash. The mixed, personalised approach features a hand-drawn ‘coach’ who provides an instant access point to learning content, acting as a host, connecting each section, and introducing the content. The character was voiced by a Senior Partner, recorded in our London studio and synched with the final product in post-production. This approach personalised the course from the start, and proved an imaginative way to convey the client’s message.

Over the course of 7 months, EY collaborated with Williams Lea on designing and developing an Academy that aimed at upskilling Assurance colleagues who have recently been promoted to New Seniors. As a result of this collaboration, we designed a curriculum using a blend of various learning modules like Virtual Instructor-Led Training, You-Tube style eBriefings and a number of digital knowledge check modalities.

We worked on designing a short opening video, which would introduce learners to the course and inject excitement right from the beginning. To do that, we knew we needed to create something a little bit different. Our designers did a fantastic job doing just that and came up with a concept of positioning project management in the context of every-day life activities like going to the cinema or weekly shopping. This, in combination with a multitude of colourful graphics, background music and a simple, sharp message, resulted in a unique audio-visual peace that creates instant interest and presents a complex concept in a simple and relatable way. It was new, fresh and unexpected!

Mary Glowacka - Senior, PAS

Project overview

Utilising our highly-skilled Design team, we produced an accessible bite-sized video around Base Erosion and Profit Shifting, or BEPS. The drawings were conceptualised not just to supplement the narration, but to stand up as miniature illustrated stories in their own right, so our digital and instructional designers worked together to craft the animated journey you see above.

It was a pleasure to work with the DLC team. We are extremely happy with the final product and look forward to showcasing this work with our clients and internal EY colleagues.
