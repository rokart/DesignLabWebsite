Project overview

The Advisory Learning Portal is a SharePoint supported web application that allows the entire advisory population (50,000+ users) to access learning materials and choose an appropriate learning journey to support their career progression. Learners are not only able to search for relevant courses but also apply personalised filters to quickly navigate to the courses that best suit their needs. Thanks to full integration with EY Leads they can access courses directly from their search – no need to launch the learning management system separately.

Using the latest front end development technologies and a creative interactive design, this web application goes further than a normal website with search functionality. It allows the learner to interact fully with the application to view their learning journey and achieve their required goals.

As well as providing our client with secure hosting within the EY network, we also developed the application for longevity – the simplicity of the design means that anyone can update and maintain the materials in the content management system with minimal training (for example, our client is currently using interns to update their site). Thanks to the insight of our developers, our client is now able to keep their product up-to-date and pertinent for their learners for many years to come.

My client really liked the Design Lab’s visual representation and site concept based on the brief – this and the production values, flexibility and response time definitely set us apart. They really liked how the site responded to learners’ searches and how intuitive the interface was. It’s a really creative way of presenting this kind of information – we definitely exceeded client expectations!

Richard Seaman - Manager, PAS

Project overview

This Flash based interactive app provided a platform for EMEIA FS Risk Leadership to connect with their teams through a more modern and easy-to-use platform. Using a ‘character line-up’ style home page, users can click on each leader to access bespoke videos.

The app allowed the leadership team to showcase their area of expertise within their sector in an accessible and interactive way – bringing the content to life and allowing their teams to connect with partners on a more personal level.

The app was used as part of a blended two hour face-to-face session which involved networking, interactive learning, video and discussion.

The feedback we received from the Partners for this app was absolutely great. They were excitedly telling everyone how they had been filmed in front of a green screen and everything was really slick! The videos were very high quality and looked really professional. Amazing!

Zahra Sheikh - Senior Manager, PAS
