Project overview

Our advanced templated option for eLearning is based around quick-to-deploy content population tools, which allow all modules to be customised to our clients’ branding while remaining templated in functionality and interactivity for speed of production. Each section of the course is templated in advance, then populated from content devised by an Instructional Designer and Subject Matter Resource, or SMR, usually provided by the client. In this example, a simple, accessible design ensured it was a high-impact, low-timescale piece of eLearning, aligned to EY’s brand identity. Project overview

Coexist was a markedly different client for Design Lab – a non-profit organisation that seeks to promote religious literacy, awareness and tolerance in the workplace. This therefore was a very sensitive undertaking, requiring constant input from the client and a completely bespoke package.

Working closely with Coexist and our EY Senior Partner, we developed a comprehensive eLearning course illustrating the challenges surrounding religious literacy and tolerance in the workplace. Each page was heavily customised to match Coexist’s strong visual identity and brand guidelines. If you would like to view this course in full, please contact us.

The Religious Literacy module was a different type of module to create compared to other ones we typically build. This is because the subject matter, was subjective and not prescribed.

This required a significant level of creativity as well as sensitivity. The designers adapted to the context well, providing suggestions for images and design, and ensuring that they were keeping within a corporate but “safe” type of branding. The end product was excellent.

The team were lovely to work with – understanding the need for flexibility and accommodating my requests. The team’s attitude has to be commended – they are always approachable and friendly and I really enjoy working with them.

Zahra Sheikh - Senior Manager, Risk
