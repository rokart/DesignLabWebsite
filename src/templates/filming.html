Project overview

Split over two locations in separate countries, our resident director was able to film the client both in our offices in Central London and in our EY office in Zurich, Switzerland. Our director travelled with a small portable pack of essential filming equipment consisting of professional grade cameras, sound equipment and a lighting kit. Once filming was completed, we edited the resulting successful pitch video together in our studio facilities in London.

Oil and Gas – Asset Reliability and Integrity Management

Time

40 days

Cost

Resources

  • Digital Designer
  • Proof reader/Editorial
  • Video/Audio expert
  • Black Magic Cinema Camera
  • Nikon DSLR
  • Green Screen Studio
  • Premiere Pro
  • DaVinci Resolve
  • After Effects
  • Adobe Audition

Project overview

Our filming with bespoke animation, as used for our Oil and Gas videos, is a step beyond our standard head and shoulders offering. Employing our highly skilled animators as well as our filming team, the core content is filmed in our state-of-the-art green screen studio, cut and edited in house, and then enhanced with animated backgrounds. These animations can convey statistics, tables, related visuals and hand-drawn characters. The end result allows us to convey supplementary information alongside the central message conveyed by the speaker.

I really like it. The narrative just comes alive with the graphics! Well done on the production.
